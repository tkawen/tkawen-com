## First Maker Comment (post within 60 seconds of going live)

```
Hi Product Hunt! 👋 Hartem here, founder of TKAWEN.

Why this exists: Every Algerian founder I know pays Stripe + Auth0
+ Twilio in dollars with a foreign credit card, hosting customer
data outside the country. For regulated industries (health,
finance, education), this isn't just expensive — it's borderline
illegal.

TKAWEN provides the same primitives, but:
✓ Data hosted in Algeria
✓ Billed in Algerian Dinar (no FX, no Visa)
✓ APIs documented in Arabic
✓ Legally registered locally (Décret 20-254 + R/C + NIF + D-U-N-S)
✓ Open source where it matters (LIQAA Meet AGPL, 4 SDKs MIT)

Today you can use:
- TKAWEN Identity for SSO + KYC
- TKAWEN Connect for video/SMS/WhatsApp
- TKAWEN Pay for DZD-native payment processing
- TKAWEN Commerce for multi-tenant e-commerce (200+ live merchants)
- TKAWEN Knowledge for LMS + verifiable certificates (4,116
  practitioners certified)
- TKAWEN Logistics for fleet + carrier integration
- TKAWEN Developer Cloud for unified API access

Sandbox is free, no credit card. Documentation at
developer.tkawen.com is in Arabic with French + English coming.

What I'd love your feedback on:
- The 7-pillar architecture — does it feel coherent or overstuffed?
- The pricing model — metered DZD per call vs flat tiers, which
  reads better for indie devs?
- The open-source split — what should be AGPL vs MIT?

The whole marketing site you're looking at renders in sub-millisecond
(check the `Server-Timing` header) because it's a single 3.6 MB Rust
binary. That's a deliberate flex — we don't just talk infrastructure,
we build it.

Happy to answer anything. Made by 1 person in Annaba, Algeria. 🇩🇿
```
