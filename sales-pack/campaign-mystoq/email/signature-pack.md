# Email signature pack — passive MyStoq promotion

Every email you send during the campaign window is free promo if your
signature includes a link. 100+ emails/week × 30% click rate = 30 extra
visitors/week at zero effort.

---

## Install in Gmail (60 seconds)

1. Gmail → Settings (gear icon) → "See all settings"
2. Scroll to "Signature"
3. Click "Create new" → name it `mystoq-launch`
4. Paste the **HTML signature** below (use the rich-text editor; Gmail
   strips most CSS, keep it minimal)
5. Under "Signature defaults" → set `mystoq-launch` for both
   "FOR NEW EMAILS USE" and "ON REPLY/FORWARD USE"
6. Save changes

## When to switch back

Once the 90-day promo window closes (2026-08-31), update the signature
to point to a regular paid landing page or remove the promo banner.
Stale promos in signatures look amateur.
