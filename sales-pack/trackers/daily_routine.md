# Founder Daily Sales Routine — 4 Hours/Day Minimum

Per [[mystoq_growth_plan_90day_2026_05_09]] — STOP BUILDING, START SELLING. This is the discipline.

## Total commitment

- **4 hours/day**, 5 days/week → 20 hours/week of pure sales
- **90 days** → 1,800 hours of sales effort
- **Target**: 50+ paying customers + 50k+ MRR DZD
- **Per-deal effort estimate**: ~36 hours total (cold + warm + close + onboarding) = 50 deals in 90 days achievable

## The 4-hour structure

### Block 1 — Outbound (09:00-10:30, 90 min)

- **09:00-09:15**: Review yesterday's `leads.csv`, identify today's targets (10 cold + 5 warm/followup)
- **09:15-10:00**: Send 10 cold WhatsApp using templates 01/06/11
- **10:00-10:30**: Send 5 follow-ups using templates 03/08/13

**Daily quota**: 10 cold + 5 follow-up = **15 messages minimum**

### Block 2 — Demos (10:30-12:00, 90 min)

- 2-3 demo calls scheduled per day
- Use LIQAA Meet (eat your own dog food!)
- 20-30 minutes per demo
- Always record (with consent) for review

**Daily quota**: **2 demos minimum**

### Block 3 — Lunch + reset (12:00-14:00)

Do NOT skip. Sales drains energy faster than coding. Eat properly.

### Block 4 — Field + closes (14:00-16:30, 150 min)

Choose ONE per day:

**Field day (Mon/Wed):**
- 14:00-15:00: drive to neighborhood
- 15:00-16:00: walk-in visit to 5 pharmacies (PharmaPro) — leave business card + 1-page brief
- 16:00-16:30: return

**Close day (Tue/Thu):**
- 14:00-15:30: handle objections from morning demos (templates 04/09/14)
- 15:30-16:30: send checkout links + close confirmations (templates 05/10/15)

**Reflect day (Fri):**
- 14:00-16:00: review week's data, what worked, what failed
- 16:00-16:30: plan next week's targets

### Block 5 — Admin (16:30-17:00)

- Update `leads.csv` with today's status changes
- Schedule tomorrow's demos in calendar
- Quick reply to any incoming WhatsApp not yet answered

## Weekly quota

| Metric | Daily | Weekly | Per 90 Days |
|--------|-------|--------|-------------|
| Cold messages sent | 10 | 50 | 600 |
| Follow-ups sent | 5 | 25 | 300 |
| Demos held | 2 | 10 | 120 |
| Field visits | 5 (M/W) | 10 | 120 |
| Closes attempted | 2 (T/Th) | 4 | 48 |
| Paid customers | — | 4-5 | **50-60** |

## Tracking

Update `leads.csv` with every status change:
- `COLD` → first message sent
- `RESPONDED` → got a reply
- `DEMO_BOOKED`
- `DEMO_HELD`
- `OBJECTION` (note which type)
- `PROPOSAL_SENT`
- `PAID` 🎯
- `LOST` (note reason — important for learning)
- `DEAD` (no response after 3 follow-ups)

## Anti-burnout rules

1. **No selling on Friday/Saturday** — your reset days
2. **No checking sales WhatsApp after 18:00** weekdays
3. **One day/week for technical work** (Friday afternoon if you must)
4. **Celebrate every PAID** — even small (post to LinkedIn, tell friends)
5. **Track losses too** — every "LOST" is data, not failure

## The math reminder (when motivation flags)

- 10 cold messages → 3 responses → 1 demo → 0.3 paid
- 50 cold/week → 15 demos/week → 4-5 paid/week
- 4-5 paid/week × 12 weeks = **48-60 paid customers**

That's 3M-5M DZD ARR from PharmaPro alone.

## The honest truth

This is harder than building. It will feel awkward. People will ignore you. Some will be rude.

The 5-10% who say YES will fund everything you've built.

The only failure mode is **stopping**.
